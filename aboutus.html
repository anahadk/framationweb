About Framation

Framation exists to help Indian and global brands win with Indian consumers.

We work with product-led businesses—across India and overseas—to shape how their offerings are positioned, perceived, and chosen within the Indian market.

By combining cultural insight, brand strategy, design craft, and digital performance, we translate product value into brand presence that feels local, relevant, and emotionally resonant.

Not just aesthetics.
Not just campaigns.
We frame Brands into Memory.

Our Story

Crafted by an eccentric techie and a marketing geek, Framation combines deep digital expertise with marketing intelligence to build a living content engine for modern brands.

We design systems that adapt with changing consumer behavior, evolving platforms, and today’s VUCA environment—allowing Indian and global product brands to stay culturally relevant, context-aware, and commercially effective in the Indian market.

Equal parts technology-driven and insight-led, Framation operates where creativity meets scalability.
